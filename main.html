Mad King of the crimson court

From who will the knives come from?

exciting social deduction game, will you be able to survive your courts schemes? will you be killed by them, or worse?

The mad king

Troubled by the tribulations of his court, the king´s mind has been twisted to the point of no return. chasing non-existent shadows and inocent criminals has started to turn friends against him and emboldened his foes will he be able to prove his minds still sharp enough to be king?

The court

comprised by the trustees of the king, this are men tasked with the most important tasks of the kingdom, and have skills fit for this responsibilities. utmost loyal, they started faltering when the king lost to the voices in his head now with the task of dethroning the king, will they be able to leverage their positions to acomplish this task?

The royal guard

cunning warriors, hand picked from the strongest of all and tasked with protecting the royal family to the last man with their unwavering oath to their duty and blade, they follow the king´s orders without questions
